Healthy Recipe of the Week

Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  4. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice into strips.
  5. In a large bowl, combine salad greens, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber.
  6. Top salad with grilled chicken slices.
  7. Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad. Toss to combine.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Health Benefits

This grilled chicken salad is a healthy choice because it incorporates lean protein from the chicken, fresh vegetables for essential vitamins and fiber, and heart-healthy olive oil and balsamic vinegar for dressing. Grilling the chicken reduces the need for added fats, making it a nutritious meal option.
